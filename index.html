Why this exists

One calm place for the things that matter to our community — instead of hunting across a dozen apps and group chats when it counts.

🛡️
Safety, centralized. Air quality, fire weather, active fires, and evacuation status pulled from several official sources and brought into one view — tuned to Tesoro Highlands, not a whole region.
🏘️
Everyday life, too. Local events for every age and the neighborhood know-how that usually lives buried in chat threads.
🤝
Honest and private. Every source is official and public — nothing invented. No accounts, no ads, no cookies — just anonymous visit counts so we can see if it's helping, nothing personal about you. And if a feed goes down, the page says so instead of faking an all-clear.
✍️
By neighbors, for neighbors. Built and curated by neighbors, and open to ideas. Spot something wrong, know a great local spot, or want a new feature? Share it in the group chat — this grows with us.

The goal: the first thing you open when something feels off — and still worth a look when everything's fine.

A neighbor project — not the official HOA, and not an official warning system. In an emergency, call 911 and follow CAL FIRE, LA County Fire, and Sheriff evacuation orders. Live fire, air, and weather data come from public government feeds and can lag or be incomplete — always confirm with official sources. Ideas for the site? Bring them to the community group chat.
